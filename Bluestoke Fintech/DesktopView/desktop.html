Frequently Asked Questions?

Find answers to common questions that come in your mind related to IPO.

How to Subscribe to an IPO? +
  • Step 1: Login to your respective service provider.
  • Step 2: Click on the IPO button.
  • Step 3: Select the IPO you want to bid and enter the relevant details.
  • Step 4: Your subscription will be completed once you make the payment or give permission.
Should I buy an IPO first day? +

This depends on your research and risk tolerance. Some IPOs may see significant growth on the first day, while others may not. It's important to evaluate each IPO carefully.

How do you know if an IPO is good? +

Consider factors such as the company's financial health, industry potential, and the offering price. It's also important to review the prospectus and consult financial experts.

How to check IPO start date? +

You can check the IPO start date on the stock exchange website or through your broker's platform. The company's website will also announce the IPO dates.

What is issue size? +

Issue size refers to the total value of shares that the company is offering in the IPO, typically mentioned in crores (₹).

How many shares in a lot? +

The number of shares in a lot can vary depending on the IPO. It is usually defined in the prospectus or by the stock exchange.
